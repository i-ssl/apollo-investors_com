Dear Home Seller:

If you are like most people, selling a home can be a very stressful process. If you are under financial pressure, emotional pressure of some kind… or you’ve tried, but just haven’t been able to get your home sold… that stress level can go way up.

We understand these problems and we make it our job to find workable, win/win solutions for folks like you.

It doesn’t matter if there is no equity in your home or there are charges against it… we will still buy it! It doesn’t matter what kind of condition it is in… we will still make you a bona fide “as is” offer.

If you are currently late on payments or in foreclosure, we can still buy your home. If you have problem tenants that you don’t want to deal with, we can take over the problem.

Our home buying solutions are designed to solve problems just like these.

HERE ARE SOME OF THE REASONS IT MAKES SENSE TO DO BUSINESS WITH US…

WE CAN CLOSE QUICKLY!

We can buy your home in 72 hours if you need us to. If you need more time, just let us know… it’s no problem. You can take as long as you like to move out… we won’t push you.

It takes an Estate agent an average of 75 days to sell your home and another 30 days to close the deal. We can work much faster.

NO HEADACHES !

You don’t have to deal with an estate agent. You don’t have to deal with banks. You don’t have to deal with unreasonable requests from buyers.

We are investors who want to buy your home, but you make the decision whether or not our offer is acceptable.

WE SAVE YOU MONEY AND PROTECT YOUR CREDIT!

On the traditional sale of a home, the Seller, on average, receives only 87% of asking price (after paying all costs – including estate agent fees). We can cut your expenses in half, or more because we do not charge a commission and there are no closing costs.

If you can’t make your payments, we can step in and keep them up to date and protect your valuable credit. The worst thing you can do to your credit report is a foreclosure. It is the thing that is the hardest to recover from.

NO RISK! NO OBLIGATION! OFFER ON YOUR HOME IN 72 HOURS!

We will make an offer on your home within 72 business hours. There is no risk. There is no obligation.

We don’t use “hard sell” techniques. If you like what we have to offer, great. If not, we will thank you for your time and get out of your hair.

We will make every effort to answer any questions you might have about our program and if you like what you hear, we’ll complete and exchange as soon as you are ready.
